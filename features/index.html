1) Calendar & Bookings

A clear day/week view with booking statuses — so your team can work calmly.

Try it for 15 days
What it is

A booking calendar where you can view by day or week — and also filter by staff/provider.

You can do things like
  • Create, edit, move, or cancel appointments
  • Track status: confirmed / cancelled / no-show / arrived
  • Block time for breaks, holidays, or closed hours
  • Reduce double bookings with a tidy staff view
What you’ll get
  • Fewer scheduling mistakes, even on busy days
  • Staff schedules feel fair and clear
  • You can see the day at a glance — no messy chats
